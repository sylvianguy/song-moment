A Song For This Moment

Have you and your loved one been fussing over which song to choose for your next important life event? Use this app to generate songs for those special moments in the fairest way possible!


Quick instructions:

This app requires you and your love partner to enter of your favourite artists. The app will then generate a playlist of random tracks from all of your favourite artists and return a random track for each relationship milestone!


Note: You will need a Spotify account to listen to the songs.
